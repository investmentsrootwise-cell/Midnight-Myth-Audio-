About

The founder and mythic architect of Midnight Myth Audio, a Black-owned creative-tech startup that fuses horror storytelling, immersive audio, and gamified lore into a living empire. He spearheaded the creation of Lashay's Midnight Myth Audio, an eBook-first affiliate-driven horror loreverse designed to empower fans, educators, and creators alike. Through modular content pipelines, branded promo kits, and classroom-ready modules, Robert transforms chilling narratives into scalable systems that affiliates can monetize and audiences can explore endlessly. His vision is simple yet expansive: to build a mythic empire where horror becomes both a story and a shared economy.
